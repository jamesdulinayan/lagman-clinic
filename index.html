Providing Medical Care for the Sickest in the Community

Pediatric Wellness

Even if your child is healthy, well-child visits are a good time to focus on your child's wellness. Talking about ways to improve care and prevent problems helps keep your child healthy.

Asthma, Emphysema, Pneumonia

Our goal is to provide relief of symptoms, prevent complications and slow the progression of the said diseases.

Diabetes Care Center

The Diabetes Care Center provides patients and their relatives a comprehensive education and patient-focused counselling on managing diabetes.

Hypertension & Cardiovascular Diseases

We provide the environment for specialized procedures in the diagnosis of patients with known or suspected heart-related symptoms and disorders.

General Internal Medicince

Experts in health promotion, disease prevention, and the care of problems both simple and complex, acute and chronic.

Complete Adult Pediatric Immunization

Immunizations are critical in protecting not only children but also adults against damaging and potentially life-threatening diseases.

EENT

Specialized unit for patients with focus on ear, nose and throat related concerns. The clinic has a seamless transition from outpatient services to referrals for more specialized procedures.

Anti Rabies Vaccine

We treat animals as a member of our family and just like humans, we want to give them the best care possible. And we will promote this through vaccination and proper information dissemination.

2021 Quadrivalent Flu Vaccine

Stay up-to-date on getting recommended vaccines. Immunization schedules for people of all ages and health care professionals to use.
